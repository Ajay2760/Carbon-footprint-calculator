Carbon Footprint Calculator
Welcome to the Carbon Footprint Calculator! This simple web application allows users to calculate their monthly carbon emissions based on energy usage, transport habits, food consumption, and waste generation. The tool helps raise awareness about personal carbon footprints and provides actionable suggestions to reduce environmental impact.

Features
Energy Emissions: Calculate CO2 emissions from electricity and gas usage.
Transport Emissions: Estimate CO2 emissions from your mode of transport (car, bike, or public transport).
Food Emissions: Track CO2 emissions based on the types of meals you consume (meat, dairy, or plant-based).
Waste Emissions: Estimate the CO2 produced from the waste you generate.
Suggestions: Get recommendations on how to reduce your carbon footprint in each category.

Technologies Used
JavaScript (Vanilla)
HTML5
CSS (For basic styling)
Prompt-based User Input

How It Works
Energy Emissions: You will be asked for your monthly electricity and gas usage. The tool multiplies your usage by fixed emission factors to calculate your energy-related emissions.
Transport Emissions: Input your vehicle type (car, bike, or public transport) and monthly travel distance. The tool calculates your emissions based on an emission factor for each mode of transport.
Food Emissions: The app asks about your weekly food habits (meat, dairy, or plant-based meals) and calculates your food-related carbon emissions.
Waste Emissions: Enter the amount of waste you generate each month, and the app calculates the emissions based on standard waste-related emission factors.
Result: The total emissions from all categories are summed up, and suggestions for reducing your carbon footprint are provided.
